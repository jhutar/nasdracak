# Subplots: Ohrožená vesnice

## SP-001: The Secret of Rypáček (Act I)
- **Function:** Introduces the tone, Pája's skills (healing the dragon), and establishes the "things are not what they seem" motif (a pig looking for truffles, not just lost).
- **Beat 1:** Babička Chrasta cries over her lost pig.
- **Beat 2:** Pája tracks the pig, talks with neighbour Sibila, finds tracks, negotiates with kids, and finds it defending a dragon.
- **Beat 3:** Pája heals the dragon, then chases the poacher.
- **Resolution:** Rypáček is returned; the truffle secret is hinted at.

## SP-002: Bělka the Lucky Goat (Act II)
- **Function:** Introduces the conflict on the meadow (mercenaries scaring animals), forces Pája to interact with Jarmil (establishing his rules), and begins the wolf-taming arc.
- **Beat 1:** Bělka is stuck on a cliff, menaced by a wolf.
- **Beat 2:** Pája tries to use the dragon's poison, fails, and must instead calm the wolf.
- **Beat 3:** Bělka unearths the dagger; Pája offends Jarmil and must make amends.
- **Resolution:** Bělka is saved, the dagger is found, and Pája learns to respect the Polevik.

## SP-003: The Wolf's Trust (Act II-IV)
- **Function:** Demonstrates Pája's thematic growth (patience vs. control) and provides her with a crucial ally for the climax.
- **Beat 1:** Initial tense encounter on the cliff.
- **Beat 2:** Pája leaves bones in the forest, the wolf is cautious.
- **Beat 3:** Pája leaves eggs, the wolf allows a touch.
- **Resolution:** The wolf attacks Vratislav to save Pája during the climax.
