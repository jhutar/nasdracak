## POV Mode Reference

<!-- Select one mode. Uncomment its block in the POV Schedule section below.
     Modes are not mutually exclusive — a braided structure can use named chapters (alternating).
     Document any hybrid clearly in the Deviation Notes below. -->

| Mode | Description | Classic Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Single POV** | One narrator throughout. All reader knowledge is filtered through a single consciousness. | *Jane Eyre*, *The Catcher in the Rye*, *Rebecca*, *Normal People* |
| **Alternating** | Each chapter belongs to exactly one named POV character. Strict demarcation. | *A Song of Ice and Fire*, *The Girl on the Train*, *Big Little Lies* |
| **Dual narrative** | Exactly 2 POV characters; often contrasting timelines, locations, or social positions. Tension built through juxtaposition. | *The Hours*, *The Guernsey Literary & Potato Peel Pie Society*, *Before I Go to Sleep* |
| **Braided** | 3+ independent storylines that develop in parallel and weave together; convergence is structural, not incidental. | *Cloud Atlas*, *The Poisonwood Bible*, *Station Eleven* |
| **Ensemble** | 4+ POVs with roughly equal weight; no dominant protagonist; individual episodes feed communal story. | *Pillars of the Earth*, *Les Misérables* (omniscient variety), *The Corrections* |
| **Mosaic** | Many short POV fragments, sometimes breaking chapter unity; close association between POV and theme rather than plot. | *Olive Kitteridge*, *A Visit from the Goon Squad*, *Jennifer Government* |
| **Frame + Embedded** | An outer narrator (or document wrapper) frames one or more inner narrators; the frame is itself a character or device. | *Wuthering Heights*, *The Princess Bride*, *House of Leaves* |
| **Chorus / Community** | A collective "we" voice narrates; individual perspectives emerge and submerge from the communal witness. | *The Virgin Suicides*, *Then We Came to the End*, *The Buddha in the Attic* |
| **First-person-multiple** | Each POV uses "I"; voices must be sharply differentiated because the pronoun offers no disambiguation. | *As I Lay Dying*, *The Poisonwood Bible*, *Gone Girl* |
